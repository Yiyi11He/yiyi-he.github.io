About

I am a PhD candidate at Murdoch University, where my research focuses on multimodal approaches in pathology — combining imaging, molecular, and clinical data to better understand disease at a systems level. I am passionate about making computational pathology methods more accessible and robust, which is why I created this space to document my research journey and share what I learn along the way.

Research & Projects

Multimodal Integration in Computational Pathology

My doctoral research investigates how combining multiple data modalities — such as whole-slide imaging, transcriptomics, and clinical metadata — can improve diagnostic accuracy and disease characterisation in pathology.

Focus: Multimodal data fusion in pathology

Institution: Murdoch University

Methods: Whole-slide imaging, transcriptomics, clinical metadata integration
